The Makuyu Education Centre is a children's home in the village of Makuyu, Kenya, committed to providing a home to underprivileged children. Founded in 2011, the children's home also provides those children with free meals every day to help fight malnutrition, and to ensure they don't learn on an empty stomach. This initiative is local, accountable, direct and efficient. 100% of our donations go to Kenya.
