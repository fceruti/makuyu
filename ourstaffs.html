Meet our executive team!

Alex, Lisa, and Pavel are responsible for running the organization. Alex is a IT professional who lives and works in Nairobi. His knowledge of Kenyan culture and proximity to the children's home is essential to the success of the project. Lisa, who resides in California, is instrumental to many of our fundraising efforts. Pavel, who lives near Washington D.C., is the original founder of the organization.
